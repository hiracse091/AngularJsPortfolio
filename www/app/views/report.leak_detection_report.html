4. Conclusion

Pressure Test - No Leak

Based on our testing methods conducted to date, we can confirm that all plumbing services tested at the property are in serviceable condition, with no leakage concerns located within the domestic hot / cold water or shower breach lines identified during the testing period.
Based on the information gathered using our methods of leak detection, we can confirm that the sighted water damages are not in any significant way caused or contributed to by the domestic water services.

Pressure Test - Leak

Based on our testing methods conducted to date, we can confirm that a pressure drop occurred during testing of the failed domestic lines.
We can confirm that all other plumbing services tested at the property are in serviceable condition, with no leakage concerns located within the passed domestic lines identified during the testing period.
Based on the information gathered using our methods of leak detection, we can confirm that the sighted water damages may be caused or contributed to by the identified domestic water services.

Spray Test - No Leak

Based on our testing methods conducted to date, we can confirm that all tiling and screens tested at the property are in serviceable condition, with no leakage concerns located within the showering areas identified during the testing period.
Based on the information gathered using our methods of leak detection, we can confirm that the sighted water damages is not in any significant way caused or contributed to by the shower wall tiling or shower screen installations.

Spray Test - Leak

Based on our testing methods conducted to date, we can confirm that a leak occurred during testing of the failed tiling and / or screens.
We can confirm that all other installations tested at the property are in serviceable condition, with no leakage concerns located within the passed tiling and screens identified during the testing period.
Based on the information gathered using our methods of leak detection, we can confirm that the sighted water damages is caused or contributed to by the shower wall tiling or shower screen installations.
